The Studio

We don’t take photographs.
We compose heirlooms — the frames your family will fight over at every reunion.

Aura Pixels Studio is a small crew of photographers, cinematographers and drone pilots who grew up inside South Indian weddings — we know when the muhurtham nears, where the grandmother in her Kanjivaram sits, and exactly when the nadaswaram peaks. We shoot three-day weddings like feature films and thousand-guest receptions without missing a single face that matters.

From the first phone call to the final frame, every kalyanam is treated as a commission, not a booking.

Before You Ask

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we book?

Wedding dates go 3–6 months out, especially in the November–February and April–May muhurtham seasons. Portrait and commercial shoots can usually be scheduled within 2–3 weeks. If your date is close, message us anyway — we occasionally have openings.

What do we actually receive?

A private online gallery of hand-edited, full-resolution photographs, plus cinematic films where booked. Weddings typically include a highlight film, a teaser for Instagram, and all curated stills from every ritual — nichayathartham to reception. Nothing is watermarked; everything is yours.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes — anywhere in India, and we love destination weddings. Travel and stay are billed at cost and quoted transparently up front.

Are your drone flights legal and insured?

Our pilots fly registered drones and handle location permissions where required. For rentals, we brief you on local rules before handover.

How does payment work?

A booking advance reserves your date; the balance is due on delivery. We'll send a simple written quote — no hidden line items, ever.
